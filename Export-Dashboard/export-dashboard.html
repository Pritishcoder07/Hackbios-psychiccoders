How insurance scoring works

Coverage adequacy

Matches the declared value and commodity risk level to available clauses (ICC A/B/C) and bespoke riders.

Trade lane appetite

Checks carrier comfort with the origin / destination pair and recent loss ratios for that corridor.

Premium efficiency

Balances total insured value, deductible appetite, and optional add-ons to keep per-shipment cost competitive.
